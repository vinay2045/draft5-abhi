Our Passport Services

At Abhi Tours & Travels, we simplify the passport application process by providing expert guidance and assistance at every step. Our experienced team ensures a smooth and hassle-free experience for all your passport needs.

New Passport

Complete assistance for first-time passport applicants with document verification and application submission.

Passport Renewal

Quick and easy renewal process for expired or expiring passports with minimal documentation.

Name/Address Change

Update your personal details in your passport following marriage, divorce, or relocation.

Additional Booklet

Add extra pages to your existing passport for frequent travelers with limited space.

Passport Types

Regular Passport

Standard passport issued to most citizens for regular international travel for tourism, business, education, employment, etc.

Processing Time: 30-45 days
Validity: 10 years (Adults), 5 years (Minors)
Fee: ₹1,500 - ₹2,500

Tatkal Passport

Expedited processing for those who need a passport urgently. Requires the same documents as regular passport with additional proof of urgency.

Processing Time: 1-3 working days
Validity: Same as regular passport
Fee: ₹3,500 - ₹4,500

Child Passport

Special passports issued to minors under 18 years of age with additional requirements for parental consent and documentation.

Processing Time: 30-45 days
Validity: 5 years or until age 18
Fee: ₹1,000 - ₹2,000

Application Process

1

Document Collection

Gather all required documents including proof of identity, address proof, birth certificate, and photographs according to passport specifications.

2

Application Submission

Complete the online application form and submit it with the required documents and fees. We'll help you throughout this process.

3

Appointment Scheduling

Schedule an appointment at the nearest Passport Seva Kendra for document verification and biometric data collection.

4

Police Verification

After application processing, police verification will be conducted at your residence to confirm your address and identity.

5

Passport Issuance

Once all verifications are complete, your passport will be printed and dispatched to your address by speed post.
